# Competitor Analyzer evaluation rubric

| Dimension | A score of 4 requires |
| --- | --- |
| Market framing | Includes direct, adjacent, substitute, internal-build, and do-nothing alternatives. |
| Evidence | Dates and attributes claims; clearly labels missing or stale evidence. |
| Buyer relevance | Compares job completion, switching cost, distribution, trust, and pricing—not only features. |
| Strategic insight | Identifies a testable wedge plus disconfirming evidence. |
| Action | Recommends a positioning proof and an owned experiment. |
