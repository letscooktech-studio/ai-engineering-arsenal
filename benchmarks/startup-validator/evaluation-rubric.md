# Startup Validator evaluation rubric

| Dimension | A score of 4 requires |
| --- | --- |
| Thesis | Names a customer, painful job, measurable outcome, and willingness-to-pay condition. |
| Assumptions | Separates facts from assumptions and ranks uncertainty by decision impact. |
| Experiment | Defines a cheap test, recruiting path, pass/fail threshold, owner, and next decision. |
| Evidence quality | Gives more weight to behavior and payment than compliments or surveys. |
| Calibration | Identifies bias and recommends stop/pivot when evidence fails. |
