# CTO Operating System: quarterly reset

## Scenario

A 12-person product team reports missed dates, a rising cloud bill, and two reliability incidents. Leadership asks for “better execution.”

## Playbook output shape

1. Establish a baseline for customer-impacting incidents, delivery lead time, spend per active customer, and current product commitments.
2. Identify missing signals before assigning blame—for example, absent service ownership or cost allocation tags.
3. Choose a constrained 90-day portfolio: reliability ownership/alerts, one spend-driver investigation, and a delivery-risk reduction slice; name what product work is deferred.
4. Assign owners, thresholds, weekly review, monthly risk review, and an escalation path.

The result is a decision cadence tied to outcomes—not a generic “improve culture” memo.
