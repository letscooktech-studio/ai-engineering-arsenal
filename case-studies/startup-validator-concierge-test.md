# Startup Validator: concierge-test decision

## Scenario

A founder wants an AI weekly-reporting product for agency owners. The only evidence is three enthusiastic conversations and a landing-page idea.

## Playbook output shape

- **Thesis:** Agency owners with recurring client reporting will pay to reduce manual report assembly if a report can be delivered in their existing format within one business day.
- **Highest-risk assumption:** Owners will connect data and pay before a self-serve product exists.
- **Next test:** Recruit five owners through warm introductions; offer a paid, manually delivered report with a stated price and a 48-hour delivery SLA.
- **Pass threshold:** At least two pay before a product demo; document their workflow, data access friction, and repeat-request intent.
- **Decision:** Build the smallest ingestion/reporting workflow only if the threshold passes. Otherwise revisit ICP or job.

This is an example of a falsifiable test, not evidence that the hypothetical product has demand.
